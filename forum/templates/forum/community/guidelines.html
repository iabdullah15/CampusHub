Community Guidelines

Our community thrives on open and respectful discussions. To maintain a healthy environment, please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Be respectful: No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
  • Avoid profanity and toxic language that detracts from constructive dialogue.
  • Keep posts relevant to the community's topic and purpose.
  • Report any content or behavior that violates these guidelines.
  • Respect intellectual property rights and refrain from posting stolen or pirated material.

Moderation Policy

Our moderation system is designed to ensure a fair and safe platform for everyone. It involves text analysis, image checks, and a tiered approach to handling warnings and rejections.

Text Moderation

We use Perspective API (by Google and Jigsaw) to analyze user-generated text. If the text content is not in English, we attempt to translate it. If translation fails, we accept the content rather than unfairly rejecting it.

Scores from multiple attributes (e.g., Toxicity, Insult, Profanity, Threat) determine whether a post is Accepted, Issued a Warning, or Rejected.

Image Moderation

We use Eden AI (Microsoft Content Moderator) to classify images as safe or NSFW. Images with a borderline NSFW score trigger a warning but can still be posted, while highly NSFW images lead to immediate rejection.

Warnings & Suspensions

Warnings: Posts that violate guidelines but are not severe receive warnings. Multiple warnings can lead to suspension if the user continues to post problematic content.

Rejections: Posts with severe violations are outright rejected. Accruing a set number of rejections (e.g., 5) results in a 7-day suspension.

If a user is suspended, they cannot create new posts until their suspension period ends. Attempting to post while suspended generates an error message.

Note: Our moderation policy is continually reviewed to ensure fairness and accuracy. We welcome feedback on how to improve the community experience.
